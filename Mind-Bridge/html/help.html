Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data and chat history completely anonymous?

Yes. When you toggle "Anonymous Mode" in the chat interface, your identity is masked from peer supporters. Only the system administrator can link accounts in severe emergency situations to save a life.

How do I book a session with a therapist?

Navigate to the Schedule tab (Calendar icon). You can filter doctors by specialization and click any available white slot on the timeline to request a booking.

What is a Peer Supporter?

Peer supporters are fellow students trained in mental health first aid. They are here to listen, offer structural support, and guide you to professional resources if needed.
